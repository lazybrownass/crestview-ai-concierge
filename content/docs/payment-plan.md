# Payment Plan

## Standard structure

Every unit at Crestview Residences is sold on the same three-part structure:

- 20% down payment at booking
- 70% in equal monthly installments over 36 months
- 10% on possession

Buyers may adjust the down payment between 10% and 30% and the installment term between 12 and 60 months. The possession payment is fixed at 10% of the unit price regardless of the chosen down payment or term. The remaining balance after the down payment and the possession payment is divided evenly across the chosen number of monthly installments.

## Worked example — Unit B-305 (2 Bed, PKR 32,400,000)

Using the standard structure (20% down, 36 months):

- Down payment (20%): PKR 6,480,000, due at booking
- Monthly installment: PKR 630,000, due on the same date each month for 36 months (installments total PKR 22,680,000, which is 70% of the price)
- Possession payment (10%): PKR 3,240,000, due when the unit is handed over

## Late payment

Installments unpaid after a 10-day grace period accrue a surcharge of 1.5% of the overdue amount per month. Three consecutive missed installments trigger a formal notice; the booking may be cancelled under the terms in the booking process document if the account is not brought current within 30 days of that notice.

## Early settlement

Buyers may settle the remaining balance in full at any time with no prepayment penalty. Request an updated settlement figure from the sales office at least 5 business days before the intended payment date.

## Currency and payment methods

All prices and payments are in Pakistani Rupees (PKR). Payments are accepted by pay order, bank transfer, or cheque made out to Crestview Residences (Private) Limited. Cash payments are not accepted for any installment above PKR 500,000.
