# Amenities

## Landscaped courtyard

A 1.2-acre landscaped courtyard sits at the center of the three towers, with mature tree planting, seating areas, and a 400-meter jogging track around its perimeter.

## Wellness

- Swimming pool (25 m, temperature-controlled) with a separate children's pool
- Fully equipped gymnasium, open 5 am to 11 pm
- Yoga and studio room, bookable in 1-hour slots through the resident app

## Family and recreation

- Covered kids' play area with rubberized safety flooring
- Community hall for private events, seats up to 120, bookable by residents
- Retail arcade on the ground floor of Tower B (grocery, pharmacy, cafe at launch)

## Convenience and services

- On-site mosque, capacity 150
- 24/7 manned security at all tower entrances and the perimeter gate
- CCTV coverage of common areas and parking
- Backup power for common areas and one outlet per unit, sized to run lighting, fans, and refrigeration during an outage
- 2 EV charging bays per tower in the basement parking, expandable on resident demand
- Basement parking: 1 assigned bay per 1-bed unit, 2 per 2-bed and 3-bed unit

## Maintenance of amenities

All shared amenities are covered by the monthly maintenance charge described in the maintenance policy document. There is no separate membership fee for the pool, gym, or community hall.
