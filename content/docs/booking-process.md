# Booking Process

## Step 1 — Expression of interest

Prospective buyers submit an expression of interest for a specific unit, either through the sales office or the website concierge. Unit availability is confirmed in real time; a unit is not held until Step 2.

## Step 2 — Token amount

A token payment of PKR 500,000 holds the chosen unit for 7 calendar days. The token is adjusted against the down payment if the buyer proceeds, and is fully refundable if Crestview cannot deliver the unit as described. It is non-refundable if the buyer withdraws after the 7-day hold without cause.

## Step 3 — Sale agreement

Within the 7-day hold, the buyer signs the sale agreement, which sets out the unit, the price, the payment plan chosen (per the payment plan document), and the possession terms (per the possession schedule document). The agreement requires the buyer's CNIC (or passport for overseas Pakistanis and foreign nationals) and, for joint ownership, both applicants' documents.

## Step 4 — Down payment

The down payment (per the buyer's chosen payment plan) is due within 15 days of signing the sale agreement. The 36-month installment schedule (or the buyer's chosen term) begins the month after the down payment clears.

## Step 5 — Confirmation

Crestview issues a confirmation letter and a payment schedule showing every installment date and amount. Buyers can view this schedule at any time through the resident portal once it is issued.

## Overseas and foreign buyers

Overseas Pakistanis and foreign nationals follow the same five steps. Payments from outside Pakistan must be routed through a normal banking channel in the buyer's name and are subject to the State Bank of Pakistan's prevailing foreign remittance regulations at the time of payment.

## Cancellation and refunds

Cancellation after the sale agreement is signed but before possession returns all paid amounts minus the token amount and a cancellation charge of 5% of the unit price. Refunds are processed within 45 business days of a written cancellation request.
