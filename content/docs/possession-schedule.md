# Possession Schedule

## Target dates

Possession is scheduled by tower, tied to construction sequencing:

- Tower A: October 2027
- Tower B: November 2027
- Tower C: December 2027

Crestview refers to this window collectively as "possession Q4 2027" in sales material. Individual unit handover dates within a tower's month are assigned on a rolling basis as fit-out inspections clear, and are communicated to buyers at least 60 days in advance.

## Construction status

As of the current sales phase, structural work is complete on Towers A and B, with Tower C at slab-and-column stage. Facade and interior fit-out are underway on Tower A. These are indicative construction milestones, not contractual delivery guarantees.

## Handover process

1. Buyer receives a 60-day advance notice with an inspection appointment.
2. Joint inspection (buyer + Crestview site team) using a standard snag list.
3. Snags are rectified within 15 business days.
4. Final possession payment (10%, per the payment plan) is due before keys are released.
5. Keys and the handover documentation pack are issued at the site office.

## Delay policy

If possession for a given tower slips more than 90 days past the target month above, Crestview waives the maintenance advance (see the maintenance policy document) for the first 6 months after actual handover. This is the sole delay remedy under the standard sale agreement; no cash compensation is offered for delays under 90 days.
