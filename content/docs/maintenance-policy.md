# Maintenance Policy

## Monthly charge

Maintenance is billed monthly at PKR 18 per square foot of the unit's covered area. For example, a 1,200 sq ft unit (B-305) is billed PKR 21,600 per month. The rate is reviewed annually each January and will not increase by more than 10% year over year.

## What it covers

- Security staffing and CCTV monitoring
- Landscaping and courtyard upkeep
- Common area cleaning, lighting, and elevator maintenance
- Backup power fuel and servicing for common areas
- Structural insurance for the buildings (not unit contents)
- Amenity upkeep (pool, gym, community hall, play area)

## What it does not cover

- Interior maintenance or repairs inside a unit
- Unit contents insurance (buyers are responsible for their own policy)
- Damage caused by a resident's negligence to common areas, which is billed separately at cost

## Maintenance advance

A one-time maintenance advance equal to 6 months of maintenance charges is collected at possession, held as a reserve against the building's maintenance fund. It is not refundable but is credited against future monthly bills once depleted.

## Billing and late payment

Bills are issued on the 1st of each month and due by the 10th. Unpaid balances after the 10th accrue a 2% monthly late fee. Access to amenities (pool, gym, community hall) may be suspended for accounts more than 60 days overdue; core services (security, elevators, backup power to the unit) are never suspended for non-payment.
