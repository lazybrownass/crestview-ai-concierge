# Frequently Asked Questions

## Where is Crestview Residences?

Canal District, Lahore. Three towers (A, B, C) around a shared landscaped courtyard.

## What unit types are available?

1, 2, and 3 bed units across Towers A, B, and C. See the listings document for current units, sizes, and prices.

## What is the payment plan?

Standard structure is 20% down, 70% over 36 monthly installments, and 10% on possession. Down payment and installment term are adjustable within limits described in the payment plan document.

## When is possession?

Target possession is Q4 2027, phased by tower: Tower A in October 2027, Tower B in November 2027, Tower C in December 2027. See the possession schedule document for the handover process and the delay policy.

## Can I resell or transfer my unit before possession?

Yes. A transfer of booking rights before possession is permitted with a transfer fee of 1% of the current unit price, payable by the outgoing buyer, and requires Crestview's written no-objection confirming the account is current. The incoming buyer completes the same CNIC/passport verification as a new booking.

## Is financing available?

Crestview does not provide in-house financing beyond the installment plan itself. Buyers are free to arrange bank financing (including Islamic financing products) independently; Crestview provides a booking confirmation letter that most banks accept as supporting documentation.

## Are pets allowed?

Yes, subject to registration and the limits in the pet policy document.

## What does the monthly maintenance charge cover?

Security, landscaping, common area upkeep, backup power for common areas, structural insurance, and amenity upkeep. See the maintenance policy document for the exact rate and what is excluded.

## Can I book a viewing?

Yes, through the sales office or by requesting a callback through the website concierge. Site visits to the show apartment are available daily except public holidays.
