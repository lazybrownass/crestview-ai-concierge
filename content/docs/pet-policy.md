# Pet Policy

## Registration

Residents may keep pets subject to registration with the building management office before the pet moves in. Registration requires proof of current vaccination and, for dogs, proof of rabies vaccination specifically. There is a one-time registration fee of PKR 5,000 per pet.

## Limits

- One pet per unit for 1-bed units
- Up to two pets per unit for 2-bed and 3-bed units
- Registration is per pet; a replacement pet requires new registration even if a prior pet is deregistered

## Breed and species

Cats, dogs, and caged birds are permitted. Dogs from breeds classified as restricted by local municipal regulations are not permitted regardless of size or temperament. Reptiles, livestock, and any species requiring a specialized enclosure are not permitted.

## Common area rules

- Dogs must be leashed at all times in common areas, including the courtyard and lobbies
- Pets are not permitted in the pool, gym, or community hall
- Owners are responsible for immediate cleanup in common areas; a cleanup fee of PKR 2,000 is charged per verified incident
- Elevators: pets should use the service elevator during peak hours (8-10 am, 6-8 pm) where available

## Noise and disputes

Repeated verified noise complaints (three within a 90-day period) result in a review by building management, which may include a requirement to board the pet during specific hours. Crestview does not evict residents over pet ownership; disputes are handled through the standard resident grievance process.
